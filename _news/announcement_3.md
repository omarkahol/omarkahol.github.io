I am thrilled to share that my first article as a corresponding author has been published in the Journal of Electrostatics. It is the end result of my master thesis in Aeronautical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano. This is a major achievement for me and I am grateful to all my friends and colleagues who supported and helped me.

In this work, we investigate Electrohydrodynamic Thrusters, a novel and promising alternative to fossil fuels for the aeronautical industry. We use dimensional analysis to derive a dimensionless theoretical model that describes how the geometrical parameters affect the thruster performance. We also conduct an experimental optimization of the cathode shape to maximize the performance.
